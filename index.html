🧠 What to take away

  • 🛍️ The crime "wave" was a property-crime wave. Shoplifting nearly tripled.
  • 🕊️ Homicide and gunfire fell from 2020 to 2023.
  • 🏙️ Downtown is the new epicenter; South LA stayed flat.
  • ⚖️ Enforcement is heavily triaged: 61% homicide clearance vs 4% property.
  • 👥 Violent victimization is split 50/50 between men and women.
